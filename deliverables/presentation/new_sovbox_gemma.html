AI Transformation Framework – powered by CRAFT

An iterative framework for AI Transformation in the Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC) [1].

Purpose: A holistic approach integrating technology, people, and processes to embed AI into the software value creation process [1].

The 5 CRAFT Dimensions

The core of the framework defining what is steered, measured, and developed [1]:

  • C – Compliance & Governance: Regulatory guardrails, policies, and EU AI Act [1].
  • R – Readiness (Human & Cultural): Skill building, role development, and psychological safety [1].
  • A – Adoption & Usage: Actual daily use across SDLC phases [1].
  • F – Flow & Process Integration: SDLC analysis and bottleneck identification [1].
  • T – Technical Enablement: Tool provision, LLM deployment, and IDE integration [1].

Discover & Iterate: The Operating System

The framework operates in 4-week iteration cycles [1]:

  • Week 1: Orientation & Planning (Pulse Check, Review, Goal Definition) [1].
  • Week 2–3: Implementation (Tool rollout, Workshops, Skills) [1].
  • Week 4: Review & Adjustment (Retro, Metrics, Readiness Gate) [1].

Pathways: Expand (new teams/phases), Deepen (better integration), or Consolidate (stabilization/change work) [1].

Three-Layer KPI Architecture

Success is measured through three distinct lenses [1]:

  1. Layer 1: Delivery Outcomes (e.g., DORA Metrics) [1].
  2. Layer 2: AI Maturity (CRAFT-Scores across 5 levels: Exploring to Evolving) [1].
  3. Layer 3: Business Impact (e.g., EBIT, Time-to-Market) [1].

Key Tools & Deliverables

  • CRAFT Explorer App: Web-based tool for Deep-Dives and Pulse Checks to generate scores and radar charts [1].
  • AI Intervention Catalog: A curated document mapping AI solutions (e.g., Code Completion, Automated Testing) to SDLC phases [1].
  • Value Stream Mapping Workshop: A 2-day process to map current state and assign AI interventions to bottlenecks [1].
  • Technical Playbooks: Setup guides from IDE plugins to LLM deployment [1].

Engagement Model Phases

A modular approach to reduce entry barriers and risk [1]:

  • Discovery (2–3 weeks): CRAFT Maturity Report and Quick Wins [1].
  • Pilot (8–12 weeks): Transforming one reference team with measurable DORA delta [1].
  • Embed & Scale (4–6 months): Scaling to 2–4 teams with a Scaling Playbook [1].
  • Continuous Partnership: Long-term strategic sparring and organizational development [1].
